Geometry Cost
Every vertex must be processed by the GPU's Vertex Shader. In VR, this happens twice (once per eye).
Topology Matters
Clean edge flow isn't just for animation; it reduces overdraw and lighting artifacts. Avoid N-gons and messy booleans.
The Golden Rule: If a polygon doesn't contribute to the silhouette or lighting, delete it.
