Occlusion Culling

Frustum vs. Occlusion
Frustum Culling hides objects outside the camera's view (automatic).
Occlusion Culling hides objects blocked by other objects (e.g., a room behind a wall). This requires pre-computation (Baking).
Setup Workflow
1
Select large objects (Walls, Floors)
2
Inspector > Static > Check Occluder Static
3
Window > Rendering > Occlusion Culling
4
Click Bake
