Lightmapping Settings
Lightmapper Progressive GPU
Lightmap Resolution 20 - 40

Max Lightmap Size 1024
Lightmap Compression High Quality
Ambient Occlusion
Checked
Directional Mode Non-Directional
Texels Per Unit Target: 20-40
This controls the quality. For VR, 40 is high quality (indoors), 20 is standard, and 10 is fine for large outdoor terrains. Don't use default (which is often too high).
Max Lightmap Size Target: 1024
Keep individual maps small to reduce loading hitches. 1024px is the sweet spot for Quest 2. 2048px is acceptable for Quest 3 but consumes 4x memory.
Compression High Quality
Always use High Quality compression. Low quality creates ugly banding artifacts in VR that are very noticeable.
Visual Debugging: Use the Draw Mode > Lightmap Resolution view in the Scene window to see a checkerboard pattern. Ensure squares are roughly consistent in size.
