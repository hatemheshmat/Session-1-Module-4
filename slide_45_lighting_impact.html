Lighting Performance Impact

Real-time Lighting
Calculated Per Frame: Every pixel's lighting is re-calculated 72-90 times per second.
Shadows are Expensive: Real-time shadows require rendering the scene multiple times (Shadow Cascades).
High CPU & GPU Cost: Heavily impacts Draw Calls and Fill Rate.
Baked Lighting
Pre-calculated: Lighting is calculated once in the editor and saved into textures (Lightmaps).
Zero Runtime Cost: Rendering a baked object is as cheap as rendering an unlit texture.
High Quality: Can support global illumination (GI) and soft shadows without performance loss.
