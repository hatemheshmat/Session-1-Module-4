Animation Culling

The Invisible Cost
By default, Unity updates every bone in a skeleton every frame, even if the character is behind a wall or behind the player. This wastes massive amounts of CPU time.
Always Animate (Default)
Updates bones even when off-screen. Avoid this.
Cull Update Transforms
Stops moving bones when invisible. Logic still runs. Safe & Recommended.
Cull Completely
Stops everything. Best performance, but can cause "pop" glitches if animation state desyncs.
