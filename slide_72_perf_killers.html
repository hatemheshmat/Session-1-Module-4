Performance Comfort Killers

Judder
Cause: Missed Frame Budget (>11ms)
Physical Effect
The world momentarily "freezes" or drags behind your head movement. This breaks the Vestibulo-Ocular Reflex (VOR), causing instant dizziness.
High Latency
Cause: Motion-to-Photon > 20ms
Physical Effect
A "swimming" or "drunk" sensation. The visual world reacts slightly later than your inner ear expects, leading to a feeling of heaviness and disorientation.
Unstable FPS
Cause: Fluctuating Workload
Physical Effect
Constant switching between 72Hz and 90Hz (or relying heavily on ASW reprojection) creates visual artifacts and unpredictable motion, which is often worse than a steady lower framerate.
Golden Rule: A rock-solid 72 FPS is always better than a jittery 90 FPS. Consistency is king.
