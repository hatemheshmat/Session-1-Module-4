Understanding Latency

Motion-to-Photon (MTP)

The time elapsed between a physical movement of the user's head and the corresponding updated visual photon being emitted from the display.

High latency creates a disconnect between the vestibular system (inner ear) and the visual system, instantly breaking presence.

< 20ms
Target Threshold
20ms Limit
Comfortable (Presence Maintained) Discomfort (Nausea Risk)
