Light Probes

Lighting Moving Objects
Baked Lightmaps only work for static objects. Moving objects (players, enemies, physics props) cannot use them. Without lighting, they look flat and detached from the scene.
The Solution
Light Probes are invisible points in space that sample the baked lighting. As an object moves, it interpolates lighting data from the nearest probes.
Performance Win
  • Cheaper than Real-time Lights
  • No Draw Call overhead
  • High quality indirect light
