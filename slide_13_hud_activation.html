Activating the HUD

Recommended
Method A: OVR Metrics Tool
1
Install the APK
Use Meta Quest Developer Hub (MQDH) or SideQuest to install OVRMetricsTool.apk.
2
Launch App
In the headset, go to App Library > Filter > Unknown Sources > OVR Metrics Tool.
3
Enable Overlay
Toggle the "Persistent Overlay" switch to ON.
Best for detailed analysis (GPU/CPU utilization, Thermal throttling).
Method B: Built-in Overlay
1
Open Settings
Go to Settings > System > Developer.
2
Toggle HUD
Find "Performance HUD" and switch it to "On".
Requires "Developer Mode" to be enabled on your Meta account and headset.
