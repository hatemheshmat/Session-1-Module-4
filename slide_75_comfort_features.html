Tunneling (Vignette)
The #1 Comfort Feature. When the player moves or turns smoothly, restrict their Field of View (FOV) with a black border. This tricks the brain into focusing on the static "cockpit" view, reducing vection and nausea.
Implementation: Use OVRVignette or a simple UI Image overlay that fades in based on velocity.
