VFX Performance Tips

Reduce Particle Count
Don't use 1000 particles when 50 larger ones will do. Use "Bursts" for impact instead of high constant emission rates.
Use Texture Sheet Animation
Instead of spawning new particles for animation frames, use a single particle with a sprite sheet (flipbook). This is much cheaper on the CPU.
Avoid Mesh Emitters
Emitting particles from a Mesh shape is expensive (CPU must calculate surface positions). Use simple Sphere, Box, or Cone shapes.
Aggressive Culling
Set "Culling Mode" to Pause and Catch Up. Don't simulate particles that are behind the player or in another room.
The Enemy: Overdraw
5x Cost
When transparent particles overlap, the GPU must calculate the pixel color multiple times per frame.

Fill Rate is the #1 bottleneck for VFX on mobile VR.
