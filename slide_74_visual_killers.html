Visual Comfort Killers

Z-Fighting (Flicker)
When two surfaces occupy the exact same position, the GPU rapidly switches between them, causing intense high-frequency flickering. This is extremely painful in VR.
FIX: Offset geometry by at least 1mm or use Decal shaders.
Aliasing (Shimmer)
Jagged edges that "crawl" or shimmer as the player moves their head. This visual noise forces the eyes to constantly refocus, causing strain.
FIX: Enable MSAA 4x in Quality Settings.
Face-Locked UI
UI elements that are perfectly locked to the camera (Screen Space) feel like "dirt on glasses." The eyes struggle to converge on them because they have no depth.
FIX: Use World Space Canvas placed 1-2m away.
Incorrect Scale
If the world scale is wrong (e.g., a chair is 3 meters tall), it creates a sensory conflict known as "Alice in Wonderland" syndrome.
FIX: Ensure 1 Unity Unit = 1 Meter. Measure everything.
