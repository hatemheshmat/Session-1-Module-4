The Shopping Analogy
Imagine buying 10 items at a store, but paying for each one separately, walking out to your car, and coming back in for the next item. That is 10 Draw Calls.
The Batching Solution
Batching is putting all 10 items in one cart and paying once. The GPU renders everything in a single command.
