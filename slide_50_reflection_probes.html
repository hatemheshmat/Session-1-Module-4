Box Projection Essential for Indoors
Off
Reflections look infinite
On
Reflections match room size
Without Box Projection, reflections in a small room look like they are miles away (like a skybox). Box Projection corrects this parallax.
Resolution Tip
Use 128px or 256px. Rough materials blur the reflection anyway, so high resolution is wasted memory.
