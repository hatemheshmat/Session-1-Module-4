Imposters and Billboards

Billboard
A simple 2D plane (quad) that always rotates to face the camera. Perfect for grass, particles, and distant clouds.
Imposter
A more advanced technique where a 3D object is baked into a 2D texture at runtime or edit time. Used for distant trees and buildings.
